Services

Metrosource LTD, within the confines of professionalism offers comprehensive consultancy services in:

Architectural

These includes design, preparation of construction documents, and construction administration, feasibility studies and architectural programming.

Project Management

The application of processes, methods, skills, knowledge and experience to achieve specific project objectives within agreed parameters.

Quantity Surveying

Our Surveyors are responsible for managing all aspects of the contractual and financial side of construction projects.

Engineering Services

We provides specialized engineering services to help clients bring products or technologies to market depending on the project scope.

Civil and Structural Engineering

This involves the design, construction, maintenance and repair of infrastructure such as roads/bridges, buildings, and underground transport systems.

Mechanical and Electrical Engineering

These encompasses the manufacture and repairing of a diverse range of electrical and mechanical products and services
