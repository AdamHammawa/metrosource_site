Welcome to MetroSource LTD

Our Story

Metrosource Limited is an indigenous firm that offers premium, comprehensive services in the design and construction industry in Nigeria. The company was founded in 1998. It is owned and managed by well experienced, creative and highly motivated professionals whose depth of industry experience are rooted in professionalism and perfection. This gives us a strong, competitive advantage in our operations in the Nigerian Construction industry.

Metrosource Limited has its headquarters located in the heart of the Federal Capital Territory, Abuja with other locations in Lagos and Yola while handling and executing projects across Nigeria and sub-Saharan Africa.
